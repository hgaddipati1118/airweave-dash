## What is Airweave?

**Airweave** transforms any data source into searchable knowledge that your AI agents can access instantly. Connect apps, databases, documents, and productivity tools to create a unified knowledge base accessible through our REST API or MCP server.

<Tip title="Perfect for AI Agents">
When using MCP, Airweave essentially becomes a semantically searchable MCP server that your agents can query across all your connected data sources.
</Tip>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Collection">
    **Set up a collection** - a logical group of data sources that gets its own searchable endpoint. Think of it as your project workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Sources">
    **Add data sources** to your collection through OAuth2 flows in our UI or API. Data syncs automatically once connected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search Your Data">
    **Query through APIs** - Use our REST endpoints or MCP server to let your AI agents search across all data in your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
